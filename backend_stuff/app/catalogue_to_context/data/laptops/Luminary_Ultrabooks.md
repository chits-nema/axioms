# Luminary Ultrabooks — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

Luminary Ultrabooks is a San Jose, California-based premium laptop brand founded in 2016. Luminary was created by a team of former Apple and Lenovo engineers with a singular mission: build the world's best ultrabooks for creative professionals. Every design and engineering decision is subordinated to three principles — uncompromising display quality, ultra-thin form factor, and exceptional battery life.

Luminary has built a cult following among graphic designers, photographers, video editors, UX/UI designers, and architects. The company sold 480,000 units in FY2025 at average selling prices exceeding $1,800 — one of the highest ASPs in the consumer/prosumer laptop market. Luminary does not compete for enterprise procurement contracts; its sales are primarily direct-to-consumer via its website and flagship stores in New York, Los Angeles, London, and Tokyo.

Luminary received Red Dot Product Design Awards in 2023 and 2025, the iF Design Award 2024, and is consistently ranked in the top 3 for display quality by Notebookcheck, RTINGS, and LaptopMag.

## Product Lines

Luminary offers three laptop lines, each available in limited configuration options (the company deliberately limits SKU proliferation to maintain quality control).

### Line 1 — Luminary Air (Thin & Light)

**Target User:** Designers, content creators, students, and business professionals who prioritize portability and battery life without sacrificing display quality.

**Form Factor:** 13.4" (Air 13) and 15" (Air 15); ultra-thin clamshell; 8.8 mm chassis depth  
**Operating System:** Windows 11 Home or Pro; Linux (Ubuntu Certified for select SKUs)

#### Luminary Air 13

**Luminary Air 13 Core — Starting Configuration**
- Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon X Elite (12 cores, up to 4.2 GHz, Qualcomm Hexagon NPU 45 TOPS, 23W TDP)
- Memory: 16 GB LPDDR5X-8448 (soldered, not upgradable)
- Storage: 512 GB PCIe Gen 4 NVMe SSD (soldered)
- Display: 13.4" OLED, 2880×1800 (2.8K), 400 nits SDR / 1000 nits peak HDR, 100% DCI-P3, VESA DisplayHDR True Black 500, 120 Hz, Corning Gorilla Glass 7
- Graphics: Qualcomm Adreno GPU (integrated)
- Battery: 58 Whr; up to 22 hours (video playback); 15 hours (Luminary real-world mixed-use test)
- Weight: 1.09 kg (2.4 lbs)
- Chassis: CNC-machined aircraft-grade 6061 aluminum; available in Midnight Slate, Silver Frost, and Rose Mist
- **MSRP: $1,299**

**Luminary Air 13 Pro — High-Performance Configuration**
- Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon X Elite (same SoC; boosted power limit to 28W in Pro mode)
- Memory: 32 GB LPDDR5X-8448 (soldered)
- Storage: 1 TB PCIe Gen 4 NVMe SSD
- Display: Same display + Luminary TrueColor calibration (factory-calibrated to ΔE<1.0 per unit)
- Graphics: Qualcomm Adreno GPU
- Battery: 58 Whr; up to 20 hours
- Weight: 1.09 kg (2.4 lbs)
- **MSRP: $1,649**

#### Luminary Air 15

**Luminary Air 15 Pro**
- Processor: Intel Core Ultra 7 258V (8 cores: 4P + 4E, up to 4.8 GHz, Intel Arc Graphics, Intel NPU 48 TOPS, 17W TDP)
- Memory: 32 GB LPDDR5X-8533 (soldered)
- Storage: 1 TB PCIe Gen 4 NVMe SSD
- Display: 15" OLED, 2880×1920 (3:2 aspect ratio), 500 nits SDR / 1200 nits peak HDR, 100% DCI-P3, VESA DisplayHDR True Black 600, 144 Hz variable, touch-enabled, Corning Gorilla Glass DX+
- Graphics: Intel Arc 140V (integrated, 8 Xe-cores)
- Battery: 70 Whr; up to 18 hours
- Weight: 1.54 kg (3.4 lbs)
- **MSRP: $1,999**

#### Air Series Key Features
- Factory display calibration: each unit tested with Luminary ColorLab; achieves ΔE<1.5 average (Pro models: ΔE<1.0)
- Dolby Vision and HDR10+ certified
- Haptic touchpad: glass surface, adjustable click pressure, 4-finger gesture zone
- Backlit keyboard: individual key illumination, 1.4 mm travel, whisper-quiet mechanism
- Webcam: 12 MP 4K with Luminary NeuralCam AI (background blur, low-light enhancement, eye contact correction)
- Speakers: quad speaker array with Dolby Atmos, tuned by Harman Kardon
- Ports: 2× USB4 Gen 3×2 (40 Gbps, DisplayPort 2.1, PD 100W), 1× USB-A 3.2 Gen 2, 3.5 mm combo; Air 15 adds microSD UHS-II
- Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be) + Bluetooth 5.4
- Windows Hello: IR + fingerprint (under-keyboard)

### Line 2 — Luminary Studio (Performance Creator)

**Target User:** Video editors, 3D artists, motion graphics designers, and photographers who need a discrete GPU but refuse to sacrifice portability.

**Form Factor:** 16" (Studio 16) only; 14.7 mm thin chassis — remarkably thin for a discrete GPU system

#### Luminary Studio 16

**Luminary Studio 16 Standard**
- Processor: Intel Core Ultra 9 285H (24 cores: 6P + 16E + 2 LP-E, up to 5.4 GHz, Intel NPU, 45W TDP)
- Memory: 32 GB DDR5-5600 (2 DIMM slots; upgradable to 96 GB by user)
- Storage: 1 TB PCIe Gen 5 NVMe SSD + 1 open M.2 2280 slot
- Display: 16" Mini-LED IPS, 3840×2400 (4K+), 1000 nits sustained / 1600 nits peak HDR, 100% DCI-P3 + 97% Rec.2020, VESA DisplayHDR 1400, 165 Hz adaptive sync (VESA AdaptiveSync), 10-bit color (1.07 billion colors), factory-calibrated ΔE<0.5
- Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 Laptop GPU (8 GB GDDR6X, 100W TGP); NVIDIA Studio Driver certified; MUX switch for direct GPU output (bypass iGPU)
- Battery: 99 Whr; approximately 10 hours (productivity); 6 hours (GPU workloads)
- Weight: 2.05 kg (4.52 lbs); 14.7 mm chassis depth
- **MSRP: $2,499**

**Luminary Studio 16 Max**
- Processor: Same Intel Core Ultra 9 285H
- Memory: 64 GB DDR5-5600 (upgradable to 96 GB)
- Storage: 2 TB PCIe Gen 5 NVMe SSD
- Display: Same panel + Luminary ColorLab Pro calibration (ΔE<0.3 guaranteed)
- Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4080 Laptop GPU (12 GB GDDR6X, 150W TGP); NVIDIA RTX Studio certified
- Battery: 99 Whr; approximately 9 hours (productivity)
- Weight: 2.05 kg (4.52 lbs)
- **MSRP: $3,199**

#### Studio Series Key Features
- NVIDIA RTX Studio certification: tested and certified for DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Blender, Cinema 4D, and AutoCAD
- Thunderbolt 4 (2 ports): 40 Gbps, up to 4× 4K displays
- HDMI 2.1 (full-size): 4K@120Hz or 8K@30Hz
- SD card reader (UHS-II)
- Full-size Ethernet (fold-out RJ-45, 2.5G)
- 4-zone RGB backlit keyboard with adjustable per-zone lighting
- Webcam: 12 MP 4K with Luminary NeuralCam Pro
- Speakers: 6-speaker array (2 tweeters + 4 woofers), Dolby Atmos, 6W output per channel
- Vapor chamber cooling (full copper, covers CPU and GPU heat zones)
- Luminary Control Center app: fan curves, display color profiles, performance presets, power modes

### Line 3 — Luminary Flex (2-in-1 Convertible)

**Target User:** UX designers, architects, and professionals who use both traditional laptop and tablet workflows, requiring a premium display and pen input.

**Form Factor:** 13.4" and 14.4" 360° convertible; 1.5 kg (13.4") / 1.72 kg (14.4")

#### Luminary Flex 14

- Processor: Intel Core Ultra 7 258V (same as Air 15)
- Memory: 32 GB LPDDR5X-8533 (soldered)
- Storage: 1 TB PCIe Gen 4 NVMe SSD
- Display: 14.4" OLED touchscreen, 2880×1920, 120 Hz variable, 500 nits SDR / 1100 nits peak, 100% DCI-P3, 10-point touch + Luminary Pen 2 compatible
- Luminary Pen 2: 4096 pressure levels, 0.25 ms input latency, tilt recognition, magnetic attachment + wireless charging on hinge
- Active Stylus Rejection: disables touch input when palm is on screen during pen use
- Battery: 66 Whr; up to 16 hours
- Weight: 1.72 kg (3.79 lbs)
- **MSRP: $2,149**

## Target Use Cases

1. **Graphic Design**: OLED display with ΔE<1.0 calibration and 100% DCI-P3 provides accurate color work in Adobe Illustrator, Affinity Designer, and CorelDRAW.
2. **Video Editing**: Studio 16 with RTX 4070/4080, 60+ GB of RAM, and PCIe Gen 5 SSD handles 4K and 8K ProRes editing in DaVinci Resolve and Premiere Pro.
3. **UX/UI Design & Prototyping**: Flex 14 with Luminary Pen 2 supports Figma, Adobe XD, and Procreate-equivalent Windows tools for wireframing and illustration.
4. **Photography Editing**: 100% DCI-P3 OLED with factory ΔE<0.5 calibration allows color-accurate retouching in Lightroom and Capture One.
5. **Architecture Visualization**: Studio 16 Max with RTX 4080 runs Enscape, V-Ray, and Lumion at high frame rates for client presentations.

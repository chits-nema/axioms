# Apex ProBook Systems — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

Apex ProBook Systems is a Taipei-based laptop and mobile workstation manufacturer founded in 1998, with North American headquarters in Austin, Texas. Apex ProBook specializes in business-grade and professional-grade laptops for enterprise IT departments, with a focus on durability, manageability, and long-term total cost of ownership (TCO). The company ships 4.2 million units annually across 62 countries and holds contracts with more than 1,400 Fortune 2000 corporations.

Apex ProBook's product philosophy is built around IT department needs: standardized hardware configurations that simplify imaging and deployment, enterprise management via Microsoft Endpoint Configuration Manager (MECM) and Intune, robust security features (TPM 2.0, BIOS-level password controls, self-encrypting drives), and extended warranty options that match enterprise refresh cycles.

The company operates its own manufacturing facilities in Taiwan and Vietnam and offers white-glove deployment services including custom BIOS configuration, asset tagging, and depot imaging at scale.

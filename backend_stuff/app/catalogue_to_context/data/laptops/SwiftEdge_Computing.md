# SwiftEdge Computing — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

SwiftEdge Computing is a Shenzhen, China-based laptop manufacturer with U.S. operations headquartered in Irvine, California, founded in 2013. SwiftEdge targets the value and mid-range laptop market with an aggressive direct-to-consumer pricing model, cutting out traditional channel markups. The company's thesis is straightforward: deliver 90% of the performance of premium brands at 60% of the price by optimizing the supply chain, maintaining a lean organization, and selling direct.

SwiftEdge shipped 2.1 million units globally in FY2025 and is particularly popular in the education sector (K-12 and higher education), small businesses, and budget-conscious consumers. The company has built a reputation for offering significant RAM and storage at price points where competitors offer less, making SwiftEdge systems popular in developer communities and with home power users who prioritize specifications-per-dollar.

SwiftEdge laptops are not sold through Best Buy, Costco, or traditional retail — exclusively via swiftedge.com and select education purchasing cooperatives (E-Rate, PEPPM, NASPO ValuePoint).

## Target Use Cases

1. **K-12 and Higher Education**: SwiftEdge Pulse and Chromabook lines offer E-Rate and PEPPM cooperative pricing; durability-tested Chromabook 11 survives classroom use.
2. **Software Development**: Nova 14 with 32–64 GB RAM and dual-SSD support runs Docker, VMs, and build environments that choke on less RAM.
3. **Budget Home Studio / Content Creation**: Apex GPU 15 with RTX 4060 enables 1080p/4K video editing in DaVinci Resolve at a price point previously unavailable.
4. **Small Business First Laptop**: Pulse 15 at $549 with 24 GB RAM and 1 TB SSD competes with $800+ laptops from name brands on raw specifications.
5. **Chromebook Enterprise (Google Workspace)**: Chromabook 14 Pro for Google Workspace organizations replacing aging Windows fleets at minimal cost.

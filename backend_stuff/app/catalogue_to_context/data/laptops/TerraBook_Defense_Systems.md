# TerraBook Defense Systems — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

TerraBook Defense Systems is a Huntsville, Alabama-based rugged laptop and tablet manufacturer founded in 2001, originally serving the U.S. Department of Defense and military branches. Over time, TerraBook expanded into adjacent sectors requiring extreme durability: energy and oil & gas, mining and resources, emergency services, construction, utilities, and transportation/logistics.

TerraBook's products are not conventional laptops pushed beyond their limits — they are purpose-engineered from the ground up for harsh environments. Every chassis, port seal, display coating, and keyboard mechanism is designed to survive conditions that would destroy a commercial laptop in minutes.

TerraBook holds significant U.S. government contracts, including the U.S. Army Tactical Computing Program and the U.S. Coast Guard Deployable Operations Laptop initiative. The company employs 780 staff, operates a U.S.-only manufacturing facility (ITAR compliant), and reported $340M in revenue in FY2025.

Certifications: MIL-STD-810H (Method 514.8 vibration, 516.8 shock, 507.6 humidity, 501.7 high temperature, 502.7 low temperature), IP66 and IP67, ATEX Zone 2 / Class I Div 2 (hazardous environment), TEMPEST Level I (Baseline), FedRAMP Moderate (cloud management console), TAA-compliant manufacturing.

### Line 1 — TerraBook Titan (Fully Rugged Laptop)

**Target User:** Military personnel, field service engineers in oil & gas environments, utility lineworkers, emergency responders, and mining operations requiring maximum environmental protection.

**Form Factor:** 14" fully rugged clamshell; 35 mm chassis depth; sealed ports with rubber door covers

**Processor Options:**
- Standard: Intel Core Ultra 5 135U (12 cores, up to 4.4 GHz, Intel NPU, vPro capable, 15W TDP)
- Performance: Intel Core Ultra 7 165U (12 cores, up to 4.9 GHz, Intel NPU, vPro capable, 15W TDP)

**Memory:** 16 GB or 32 GB DDR5-4800 (2 DIMM slots; field-replaceable with gloves)

**Storage:**
- Primary: 512 GB or 1 TB PCIe Gen 4 NVMe SSD (shock-mounted)
- Secondary: Optional secondary SSD bay (hot-swap capable on T14 Pro)
- Optional: removable SSD in locked, field-removable caddy (for classified data compartmentalization)

**Display:**
- 14" IPS LCD, 1920×1080 (FHD), 1,400 nits brightness (sunlight-readable)
- Optical bonding (eliminates internal condensation, improves sunlight visibility)
- anti-reflective and anti-scratch coating
- Glove-touch compatible (10-point): responds to thick work gloves
- Optional heater film for -20°C operation (cold weather kit)

**Operating Temperature:** -29°C to +63°C operating; -46°C to +71°C storage

**Graphics:** Intel Arc Graphics (integrated)

**Battery:**
- Primary: 73 Whr internal hot-swap battery
- Optional: Secondary external hot-swap battery (adds 73 Whr); TerraBook's dual-battery hot-swap allows field battery replacement without powering down — zero downtime
- Estimated runtime: 12 hours (single battery); 22+ hours (dual battery)

**Weight:** 2.86 kg (6.3 lbs) with single battery

**Durability Ratings:**
- MIL-STD-810H: vibration, shock, humidity, altitude, temperature (all methods passed)
- IP66 and IP67 (full dust ingress protection; 30-minute immersion at 1 meter)
- ATEX Zone 2 / IECEx / Class I Division 2 (certified safe for use in explosive atmosphere environments — oil refineries, chemical plants, mines)
- 1.8-meter drop test (6 faces, 4 edges, 4 corners = 14 drops)

**Ports (all sealed with positive-latch doors):**
- 2× USB-A 3.2 Gen 2 (sealed)
- 2× USB-C (USB4 / Thunderbolt 4, sealed)
- 1× HDMI 2.0 (sealed)
- 1× RJ-45 (sealed, Gigabit Ethernet)
- 1× RS-232 Serial (DB-9, optional, replaces optical drive bay)
- 1× SD card reader (sealed, UHS-II)
- 1× Smart Card reader (CAC/PIV)
- 1× SIM card slot (4G LTE Cat. 20 WWAN included)
- Optional dedicated GPS (L1/L5 dual-band GNSS)

**Pricing:**

# VelocityBook Pro — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

VelocityBook Pro is a Copenhagen, Denmark-based laptop brand founded in 2019 by a team of ex-Lenovo ThinkPad engineers and environmental scientists. VelocityBook Pro occupies a deliberate market position at the intersection of high performance and sustainability — every laptop is built with a minimum of 50% post-consumer recycled (PCR) materials in the chassis, runs on 100% renewable energy-powered manufacturing, and is certified carbon neutral (Scope 1, 2, and 3) via Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi).

VelocityBook Pro appeals strongly to ESG-conscious enterprise customers, particularly those in the financial services, consulting, and technology sectors where corporate sustainability commitments influence procurement decisions. The company also holds ISO 14001 (environmental management) and ISO 50001 (energy management) certifications and publishes an annual Product Environmental Impact Report covering materials, manufacturing energy, end-of-life recycling rates, and supply chain emissions.

VelocityBook Pro sold 310,000 units in FY2025 and has grown 85% year-over-year since launch. The company operates in 40 countries through a network of certified sustainable resellers and a direct corporate sales program. With an average selling price of $1,450, VelocityBook serves premium business and prosumer segments.

Certifications: ISO 14001:2015, ISO 50001:2018, EPEAT Gold (all models), Energy Star 8.0, TCO Certified Generation 9, Blauer Engel (Germany), Nordic Swan Ecolabel, SBTi-validated carbon neutral.

## Sustainability Reporting for Enterprise Customers

VelocityBook Pro provides enterprise customers with quarterly **Carbon Footprint Reports** covering their device fleet:
- Per-device Scope 1/2/3 emissions during use phase (based on actual runtime and regional grid intensity)
- Fleet-wide recycled content percentage
- Projected end-of-life recycling credits when devices are returned via Take-Back
- Exportable data for GRI Standard reporting, CDP disclosure, and CSRD (EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive) compliance

This reporting is provided at no cost to customers with 100+ active devices.

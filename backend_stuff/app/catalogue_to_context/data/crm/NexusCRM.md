# NexusCRM Enterprise — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

NexusCRM Enterprise is a New York-based enterprise CRM and customer experience platform founded in 2003. As one of the older vendors in this space, NexusCRM has accumulated two decades of enterprise customer deployments, making it particularly strong in financial services, insurance, wealth management, and banking — sectors that demand deep compliance, complex product hierarchies, and integration with core banking and policy administration systems.

NexusCRM serves 1,100 enterprise customers, the majority with 500–50,000 employees, across 29 countries. The company employs 3,800 staff and reported $720M in ARR in FY2025. NexusCRM is publicly traded (NYSE: NCRM) and has completed 11 acquisitions over its history, including Relation360 (marketing automation, 2019), WealthTrack CRM (private wealth, 2021), and ClaimPath (insurance, 2022).

Certifications: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, ISO 22301, PCI DSS Level 1, FedRAMP Moderate, FINRA-aligned, SEC 17a-4 compliant, HIPAA BAA available.

## Product Overview: NexusCRM Customer Intelligence Suite

The **NexusCRM Customer Intelligence Suite** is a modular, enterprise-grade CRM platform covering five interconnected disciplines: Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Financial Services Cloud, and Analytics & AI. Unlike horizontal CRM vendors, NexusCRM has built deep industry-specific data models for financial services, insurance, and wealth management — including household relationship modeling, AUM tracking, policy administration integration, and FINRA-compliant communication archiving.

### Key Differentiators

- **Financial Services Data Model**: Native support for Households, Advisors, AUM, Investment Accounts, Insurance Policies, Claims, and Beneficiaries — not generic CRM objects forced into financial use cases.
- **Relation360 Marketing Automation**: Built-in enterprise marketing automation with journey orchestration, lead scoring, ABM (account-based marketing), and multi-channel campaign management — acquired and fully native since 2019.
- **Regulatory Communication Archiving**: All advisor-client communications (email, SMS, WhatsApp Business, chat) automatically archived with immutable audit trails for FINRA, SEC, and GDPR compliance.
- **NexusAI Decision Engine**: Embedded AI/ML models for next best action (NBA), churn prediction, cross-sell/upsell propensity, and case routing — trained on financial services industry data.
- **Real-Time 360° Client View**: Aggregate data from core banking, portfolio management (Advent, SS&C Advent Geneva, Orion), custodian feeds, and CRM activity into a single client timeline.

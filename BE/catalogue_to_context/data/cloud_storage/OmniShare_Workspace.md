# OmniShare Workspace — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

OmniShare Workspace is a Berlin-based cloud storage and team workspace company founded in 2016, purpose-built for creative and media-production teams. OmniShare recognized that traditional enterprise file storage tools fail creative workflows — they impose size limits on video files, lack media-specific preview features, slow down review-and-approval cycles, and lack integrations with Adobe Creative Cloud, DaVinci Resolve, and Figma. OmniShare was built to close those gaps.

OmniShare serves 7,600 organizations globally — primarily advertising agencies, video production companies, architecture firms, game studios, and marketing departments — with a combined stored asset base of 42 petabytes. The company employs 290 people and generated $95M in ARR in FY2025. OmniShare has offices in Berlin, New York, and Singapore, and is backed by Accel Partners and Balderton Capital.

Certifications: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, CCPA.

## Product Overview: OmniShare Creative Cloud Workspace

The **OmniShare Creative Cloud Workspace** is a cloud storage and digital asset management (DAM) platform designed for the end-to-end lifecycle of creative assets — from raw capture and editing, through review and approval, to final delivery and archive. It combines high-capacity cloud storage, real-time media preview and annotation, client review portals, brand asset libraries, and delivery CDN into a single integrated platform.

### Key Differentiators

- **Native Media Preview**: Frame-accurate in-browser video playback (up to 8K) with no transcoding required for ProRes, RED R3D, ARRIRAW, H.264, H.265, VP9, and AV1. Audio waveform viewing. RAW photo support (NEF, CR3, ARW, DNG).
- **Frame-Level Annotation**: Reviewers can leave time-stamped comments pinned to specific frames in a video or regions of an image — no more ambiguous email feedback.
- **Adobe Creative Cloud Panel**: Direct integration allows Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign users to upload/download assets and receive feedback without leaving Adobe apps.
- **Approval Workflow Engine**: Multi-stage review and approval pipelines with version comparison, approval tracking, and automated notifications.
- **Brand Portal**: Create a self-serve, searchable library of approved brand assets (logos, fonts, templates, photography) for internal teams and external partners to access — ensuring brand consistency.
- **Delivery CDN**: Deliver final video content to clients or platforms via OmniShare's integrated CDN with custom streaming URLs and password protection.

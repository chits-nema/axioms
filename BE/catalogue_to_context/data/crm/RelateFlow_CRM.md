## Product Overview: RelateFlow Sales CRM

The **RelateFlow Sales CRM** is a lightweight, fast, and beautifully simple CRM for small sales teams. It focuses on what salespeople actually do all day: follow up, track conversations, manage deals, and get introduced to the right people. RelateFlow strips away complexity and delivers a tool that reps actually use — addressing the industry-wide adoption problem that makes many CRM implementations fail.

### Key Differentiators

- **Automatic Contact Enrichment**: Paste in a name or email address; RelateFlow auto-populates the contact with LinkedIn profile photo, job title, company size, LinkedIn URL, and Twitter handle — no manual data entry.
- **Smart Inbox**: Unified view of all emails (Gmail/Outlook), calls, notes, and tasks for every contact — RelateFlow automatically associates emails and calendar events to the right contact without any rep action.
- **Pipeline Nudge**: If a deal hasn't moved in N days (configurable), RelateFlow sends a gentle nudge to the rep's inbox — "You haven't reached out to Acme Corp in 12 days." The most-loved feature among RelateFlow customers.
- **One-Page Deal Summary**: Every deal has a single, scrollable page showing all context: company info, contact history, emails, call notes, documents, quotes, and next actions — no need to navigate between tabs.
- **Relationship Strength Indicator**: Visual indicator (hot/warm/cold) on every contact based on recency and frequency of communication — helps reps prioritize their book of business.

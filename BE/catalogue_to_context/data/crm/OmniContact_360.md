# OmniContact 360 — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

OmniContact 360 is a Minneapolis-based customer relationship management company founded in 2008, specializing in omnichannel contact center CRM and customer service automation. OmniContact 360 was purpose-built for organizations where customer service is a primary function rather than a secondary sales support tool — think insurance companies, utilities, telecommunications providers, healthcare payers, and government agencies where contact center agents handle thousands of inbound interactions per day.

OmniContact 360 serves 640 enterprise organizations globally, with an average customer size of 3,200 agents. The company employs 2,100 staff and reported $440M in ARR in FY2025. OmniContact 360 is consistently positioned in the Leaders quadrant of analyst reports for Contact Center CRM and Customer Service Software.

The company's flagship differentiator is its **AgentAssist™** platform: a real-time AI copilot for contact center agents that listens to live calls, suggests knowledge base articles, surfaces the next best action, detects customer emotion, and generates auto-summaries after each interaction — reducing average handle time (AHT) by 34% in documented customer deployments.

Certifications: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, ISO 22301, PCI DSS Level 1, HIPAA BAA available, FedRAMP Moderate, FISMA Moderate.

## Product Overview: OmniContact 360 Customer Service Platform

The **OmniContact 360 Customer Service Platform** is an enterprise-grade CRM designed for high-volume contact center and customer service operations. It centralizes all customer interaction channels (voice, email, live chat, SMS, social media, web self-service, and WhatsApp Business), manages cases from intake through resolution, and embeds AI assistance directly into the agent desktop.

### Key Differentiators

- **True Omnichannel**: Single conversation thread across channels — customer starts via chat, continues via phone, and follows up via email; agent sees one continuous timeline, not three separate tickets.
- **AgentAssist™ AI Copilot**: Real-time AI assistance during live calls and chats — surfaces relevant knowledge articles, compliance scripts, and next best actions as the conversation unfolds; auto-generates post-call summaries; reduces after-call work (ACW) by 61%.
- **Intelligent Case Routing**: AI-powered routing assigns cases to the right agent based on topic, sentiment, language, case history, and agent skill profile — not just queue position.
- **Customer 360 Sidebar**: Every interaction opens a persistent sidebar showing the customer's full history: past cases, purchase history (via CRM/ERP integration), account health score, open items, and known preferences — available to the agent in <500 ms.
- **Voice of the Customer (VoC) Analytics**: Every interaction is transcribed and analyzed for CSAT prediction, sentiment trend, emerging complaint topics, and agent performance metrics — without requiring manual surveys.

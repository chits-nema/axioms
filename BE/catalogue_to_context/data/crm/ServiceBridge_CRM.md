# ServiceBridge CRM — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

ServiceBridge CRM is an Atlanta-based customer relationship management and field service management company founded in 2014, purpose-built for service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, pest control, landscaping, commercial cleaning, and home services. ServiceBridge recognized that traditional CRMs designed for B2B software sales workflows are fundamentally misaligned with service business realities: recurring service agreements, dispatch and scheduling, technician mobile apps, job costing, and equipment service history.

ServiceBridge serves 8,700 service businesses ranging from owner-operators to franchise networks with 500+ technicians. The company employs 520 staff and reached $130M in ARR in FY2025. It has processed 42 million service jobs since inception and manages over 3.1 million recurring maintenance contracts on behalf of its customers.

ServiceBridge is used by some of the largest home services franchise networks in North America, serving as the system of record for both franchisee operations and franchisor reporting.

---

## Product Overview: ServiceBridge Field Service CRM

The **ServiceBridge Field Service CRM** unifies customer management, scheduling and dispatch, technician mobile workflows, recurring service agreement management, quoting, invoicing, and customer communication into a purpose-built platform for service businesses. It replaces the common combination of a generic CRM + a spreadsheet dispatch board + QuickBooks + a paper-based customer file.

### Key Differentiators

- **Drag-and-Drop Dispatch Board**: Visual scheduling interface showing all technicians' calendars, availability, and geographic location; assign and reschedule jobs by dragging them to the right technician and time slot.
- **ServiceAgreement Engine™**: Manage recurring maintenance contracts with automated scheduling, renewal reminders, contract billing, and equipment service history tracking.
- **Technician Mobile App**: iOS and Android app for field technicians — view job details, navigate to job site, complete checklists, collect signatures, upload photos, create quotes, accept payments, and close jobs in the field with or without internet connectivity.
- **Customer Communication Hub**: Automated job confirmation, technician-on-the-way notifications (with real-time GPS tracking link), satisfaction surveys, and review request sequences — all white-labeled with your company branding.
- **Job Costing and Profitability**: Real-time job cost tracking against materials, labor, and overhead; technician time logged automatically via mobile app; margin analysis by job type, technician, and customer.

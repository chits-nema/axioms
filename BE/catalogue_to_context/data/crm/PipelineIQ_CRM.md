# PipelineIQ CRM — Product Catalog

## Company Overview

PipelineIQ CRM is a San Francisco-based customer relationship management software company founded in 2012. PipelineIQ was built for revenue operations professionals who believe CRMs should surface insights and drive action — not just store contacts. The platform combines traditional sales pipeline management with AI-powered deal intelligence, conversation analytics, and revenue forecasting that actually predicts outcomes rather than simply rolling up rep-entered data.

PipelineIQ serves 5,400 B2B companies across the technology, professional services, and manufacturing sectors, with a sweet spot of 50–5,000 employees. The company employs 1,100 staff, including a 180-person AI research team, and reported $280M in ARR in FY2025. PipelineIQ is backed by Insight Partners and Tiger Global.

The platform's flagship AI feature, **DealSense™**, analyzes emails, calendar events, call transcripts, and CRM activity to produce per-deal health scores and forecast risk flags — helping sales leaders identify at-risk deals before they are lost.

## Product Overview: PipelineIQ Revenue Intelligence Platform

The **PipelineIQ Revenue Intelligence Platform** is a cloud-native CRM designed for B2B sales teams. It covers the full revenue cycle: lead management, contact and account management, opportunity pipeline, quote-to-cash (CPQ-lite), customer success, and revenue forecasting — unified by an AI layer that turns raw activity data into actionable revenue intelligence.

### Key Differentiators

- **DealSense™ AI**: Composite deal health score (0–100) synthesized from email sentiment, meeting cadence, stakeholder engagement breadth, competitive signals, and historical win/loss patterns. Updated hourly.
- **Conversation Intelligence**: Native call recording, transcription (22 languages), and analysis — automatically logs calls to CRM records, extracts action items, competitor mentions, and MEDDIC/BANT qualification signals.
- **Forecast Intelligence**: Bottom-up and AI-generated revenue forecasts with confidence intervals; scenario modeling; CRO/VP dashboard with drill-down by rep, team, region, and product line.
- **Mutual Action Plans (MAPs)**: Collaborative buyer/seller plan shared via a live URL — both parties track milestones, assign tasks, and see mutual progress, driving deal velocity.
- **Revenue Operations (RevOps) Hub**: Pipeline hygiene alerts, data quality scoring, process compliance monitoring, and territory and quota management.
